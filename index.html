About

45.346.987 monthly listeners

Queen is a British rock band that rose to fame in the 1970s and 1980s, known for their flamboyant style, intricate compositions, and powerful live performances. The band was formed in 1970 in London by guitarist Brian May, drummer Roger Taylor, bassist John Deacon, and lead vocalist Freddie Mercury. Queen's early sound was heavily influenced by hard rock and progressive rock, but they also incorporated elements of pop, funk, and classical music into their songs.
